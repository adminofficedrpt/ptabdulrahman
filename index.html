About

Dr. P.T. Abdul Rahman is a renowned educator and humanitarian whose legacy is rooted in uplifting communities through Quranic education and service. As the founder of Darul Quran Abdulla Academy, he has dedicated his life to creating an environment where young minds are nurtured with purpose, discipline, and divine knowledge.
